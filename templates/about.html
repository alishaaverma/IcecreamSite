About Us

Welcome to Alisha IceCreams, where every scoop tells a story! Founded in 2010 in the heart of Tamil Nadu, we are passionate about crafting the finest ice cream using only the freshest, high-quality ingredients. At Alisha IceCreams, we believe that ice cream is more than just a dessert; it’s a joyful experience. Our founder, Alisha Verma, started this journey with a simple mission: to bring smiles to faces through delightful flavors and unique combinations. Our ice cream is made in small batches, allowing us to maintain the highest standards of quality and creativity. From classic favorites to innovative seasonal specials, there’s something for everyone. We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, supporting our community and ensuring that each scoop is rich in flavor and character. Join us in celebrating the love of ice cream! Whether you’re indulging in a cone on a sunny day or enjoying a sundae with friends, we invite you to create sweet memories with us.

Thank you for being part of our ice cream family!
